Features

A complete picture of your diet and wellness — privately, on your device.

Today Tab

Your daily command center

Everything you need to log a day — meals, exercise, health notes, weight, and a quality score.

📊

QUAL Score

Rate your diet quality for the day on a 0–100 scale. Color-coded green (excellent), lime (good), amber (watch), or red (poor). A single number that tells the story.

🏆

Sweets & Snacks Tracker

Log your daily "indulgence servings" against a configurable weekly budget (default 12). The app shows weekly totals with color-coded bars — at a glance you know where you stand.

🍽️

Meal Notes

Four flexible meal slots — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks. Write what you ate in plain language. No food database hunting, no macro arithmetic. Just awareness.

🏃

Exercise Log

Log any number of activities per day. Distance-based (Walk/Hike, Run, Bike) or duration-based (Gym, Yoga, Work, Other). Supports custom icons and labels for each session.

📝

Health Notes & Tags

Attach freeform health notes and select from custom symptom/mood tags you define in Settings. Felt Great, Knee Pain, Poor Sleep — track what matters to you.

⚖️

Weekly Weight

Log your weight once a week. The app stores it per week-start date and builds a trend chart over time — without moralizing about whether the number went up or down.

Month Tab

See the whole month at once

A calendar and weekly bars that reveal patterns across 4–5 weeks.

📅

QUAL Calendar

Every logged day shows a color dot (green → red by tier) and your S&S count. Missed days are visible at a glance — a quiet motivator to stay consistent.

📈

Weekly Summary Bars

Horizontal bars for average QUAL and total S&S, by week. Each bar is color-coded against your budget so you can spot the good weeks and the rough ones.

⚖️

Weight Trend Chart

An SVG line chart of your weekly weights for the month. Line breaks where data is missing — no interpolation. Neutral blue throughout, no directional bias.

🏃

Exercise Sparklines

Mini bar charts for each exercise type — one bar per week in the month. The number beside each row shows your monthly total for that activity.

📄

Weekly Report

Generate a beautiful 7-column landscape PDF — one column per day — with meals, exercise, health tags, and scores. Share via AirDrop, Mail, or print to PDF.

🔍

Correlations

After 30+ days of logging, D³ shows detected patterns between the foods or medications you track and your health tag outcomes. Silent until it has something real to say.

History Tab

Long-term trends, three ways

Trends, Calendar, and List views — zoom out to see your arc over months and years.

📊

Trends View

Sparkline charts for Diet (QUAL + S&S), Weight, Exercise, Health Notes, and Correlations. Switch between 3M, 6M, 1Y, and ALL. Each bar represents one week.

🎨

Color-coded Bars

QUAL bars are green/lime/amber/red by tier. S&S bars are green when under budget, amber when slightly over, red when well over. Health Notes bars are a neutral blue — frequency data, no judgment.

📅

Calendar View

Navigate any month you've logged in. Tap any day to jump to that day's entry in the Today tab.

📋

List View

A searchable, filterable chronological list of every logged day. Search meal notes by keyword or filter by any health tag.

📤

History Report

Export a landscape PDF summary of your full selected date range — QUAL and S&S by week, exercise by type, health note frequency. Share with your healthcare provider.

🔍

Correlations in History

The same correlations engine runs in History Trends, using all your logged data regardless of the selected range window.

Correlations Engine

Patterns that emerge over time

D³ analyzes your logged data to surface meaningful relationships — automatically, privately, on-device.

🍽️

Food Correlations

Mark a food attribute as "watching" in Settings. D³ tracks days when that food appeared in your meal notes and compares your health tag rates — exposed days vs. baseline — using a configurable lag window.

💊

Medication Correlations

Log medication changes in Settings. After sufficient data accumulates (lag period + 7 days), D³ compares symptom rates before and after the change. Requires baseline data from before the change to be meaningful.

🎯

Statistical Gates

Correlations only surface when there's enough data to be meaningful — at least 30 logged days, a minimum exposure count, and a ≥15% difference between exposed and baseline rates. Silent when data is thin.

Customization

Tailored to you

D³ adapts to your lifestyle, not the other way around.

🎨

Accent Colors

Choose from eight accent presets (Forest, Teal, Purple, Pink, Orange, Blue, Red, Sky) to personalize the app's look.

🌙

Dark & Light Mode

Follows your device setting automatically, or override manually in Settings. True dark theme — not just dimmed.

🏆

S&S Budget

Configure your weekly Sweets & Snacks budget — total and per-category (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks). Default is 12 servings per week.

🏷️

Custom Health Tags

Create any symptom, mood, or activity tags that matter to your situation — from "Knee Pain" and "Brain Fog" to "Meditation" and "Gratitude."

🏃

Exercise Types

Customize exercise icons and add activity types that match your workout routine.

⚖️

Weight Units

Log and display weight in lbs or kg — set once in Display Preferences and it applies everywhere.
