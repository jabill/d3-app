What D³ Does

Everything you need to understand your diet

Log, score, and analyze your nutrition and wellness habits — all in one place, all on-device.

📊

Daily Quality Scoring

Rate your diet quality (QUAL) each day. A single score that summarizes how well you ate, with weekly averages and visual trends.

🏆

Sweets & Snacks Budget

Track your weekly indulgences against a configurable budget. Green when you're under, amber when close, red when over.

🍽️

Meal Notes

Log breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks as free-form notes. No calorie counting — just awareness of what you ate.

🏃

Exercise Logging

Track walks, runs, bike rides, gym sessions, yoga, yard work, and more — by distance or duration.

📝

Health Notes & Tags

Log how you feel with custom symptom and mood tags. Felt Great, Knee Pain, Low Energy — you define what matters.

⚖️

Weight Tracking

Log weekly weight and view a neutral trend chart — no green for loss or red for gain, because everyone's goals are different.

🔍

Correlations Engine

After 30+ days of logging, D³ automatically detects patterns between foods, medications, and your health outcomes.

📈

Trends & History

Sparkline charts for diet quality, S&S, exercise, weight, and health notes across 3 months, 6 months, a year, or all time.

📄

Shareable Reports

Export beautiful weekly, monthly, and trends PDF reports — perfect for sharing with a doctor, dietitian, or personal trainer.

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your phone

D³ was built from the ground up with privacy as a core principle — not an afterthought.

📵

No Internet Required

D³ works completely offline. All data is stored locally on your device using your phone's built-in secure storage.

🔒

No Accounts

No signup, no login, no email address. Your health data belongs to you — not to a tech company.

📊

No Analytics

We don't track which features you use, how long you spend in the app, or anything else. Zero telemetry.

💎

No Ads, No IAP

D³ is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. No subscription required, ever.
